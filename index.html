😁 Fourth Annual RV Trip 😁

Oct 4-9, 2017

🍆 It began as a bachelor party

We rented an RV.

We pointed its nose east.

We drove.

We saw the world's largest Yo-Yo and a statue of Kevin Costner carved out of a single log.

It was wonderful.

The next year, we had no bachelor party to plan. Then we realized we didn't need an excuse for an awesome roadtrip with friends!

The annual RV trip was born.

🚌 This year we're going bigger

Every year we discuss how to make the RV Trip even more fun. This year our big experiment is multiple RVs.

Before, attendance was limited by the number of people we could legally cram into an RV. This year, we’re opening up the headcount for everyone who wants to join the adventure.

We'll rent as many RVs as we need.

Join Us

The nature of the RV Trip makes it hard to give a lot of specifics about what we'll be doing. A big part of the fun is to only have some basic ideas of what to do, and even those only get half done. There are some constants though:

🎈 What You Can Expect

Best put, it's a 5-day party bus.

We drink, we play games, we stop at crazy places, we gamble in casinos, we eat at questionable highway restaurants, we swim in strange lakes.

This year, we are pointing the caravan in the general direction of Bend, OR. We're going to punch up to the Bend area the first night, and then spend the rest of the days exploring all sorts of places in that part of the state.

💰 What It Costs

On average, past RV Trips have cost about $600 per person for everything. This includes all food for 5 days, the RV rental, gas, activities, etc etc.

This year, we're requiring everyone to kick in a non-refundable deposit of $200. This is to ensure we are getting an accurate headcount of attendees so we can plan logistics, rent RVs well in advance, etc.

Send the deposit to Greg. Deposits will be tracked in the group Splitwise along with all other RV Trip expneses so we can easily settle up after the trip is over.

📅 When

We leave on October 4th and return late on October 8th. You'll need Wed-Fri off work.
